Vinification

All the fruit is destemmed and pressed immediately, the most ferment in temperature controlled stainless steel tanks at 16-18°C. The wine stays on the leesfor eight months before going into bottle.

Tasting & Tours

We will take you for a walk among our vineyards, where between the rows you can enjoy unique views. Harmony with nature means that time flows on a human scale, even in the cellar, where you will come into contact with the ancient rural rhythms.You can taste wines deriving form our native Friulian vines and different ones being originated form the international Merlot grape variety. Intense red wines characterised by significant peculiarities, but above all full-bodied, soft and rich white wines.
